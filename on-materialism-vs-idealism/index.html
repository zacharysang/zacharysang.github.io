On Materialism vs. Idealism

  • Idealistic projections are based on ideals and what ought to be based on core principles
  • Materialistic projections are based on what has occured in the past
  • Societal evolution is driven by some idealistic projections in spite of others
  • Societal evolution is stagnated by the materialistic belief that what has been will continue to be
